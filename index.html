Research Interests

I am currently under the supervision of Prof. Paris Perdikaris working on topics about Physics-Informed Neural Network, a branch of the multitask learning model family, and its optimization. I have been using various equality and inequality constrained optimization techniques on Physics-Informed Neural Network. I am also involved in a DeepONet project.

Besides applied mathematics and mechanics, I am interested in using machine learning to model the physics in mass spectroscopy and liquid chromatography to enhence the sensitivity and interprebilities.
