Research Interests

I am currently under the supervision of Prof. Paris Perdikaris and working with a broad interest in attention related dynamic parameter learning models and their applications. I am interested in tayloring the state-of-the-art models on standard benchmark to fit the demands of particular fields such as biomedical and engineering, where the data distributions differ slightly from the standard benchmark that the SOTAs were evaluated.
