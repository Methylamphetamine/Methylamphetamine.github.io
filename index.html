About

I am a second year master student at Applied Mathematics and Computational Science, department of mathematics, the University of Pennsylvania. I will graduate in May 2021, and then switch to the Ph.D. track of the same program immediately afterwards.

Research Interests

I am currently under the supervision of Prof. Paris Perdikaris working on topics about Physics-Informed Neural Network and its optimization. I am also involved in a Physcis-Informed DeepONet project.

Besides applied mathematics and mechanics, I am interested in using machine learning to model the physics in mass spectroscopy and liquid chromatography to enhence the sensitivity and interprebilities.
